# AIStock Token (AIS) – Tokenomics

AIStock Token (AIS) is an ERC‑20 utility token deployed on the Arbitrum One network. It is designed to serve as a payment medium for AI‑related services, enabling fast, low‑cost on‑chain settlement for workloads, usage‑based billing, and credit purchases. AIS has a fixed supply, no minting, and no vesting or lockup mechanisms.

## 2. Supply Characteristics

AIS has a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens, all of which were created at deployment. There are:

- No mint functions  
- No burn functions  
- No upgradeable proxy pattern  
- No vesting or lockup schedules  
- No team or advisor allocations  
- No private or public presale  

All tokens are freely circulating and verifiable on Arbiscan.

AIS is used as a payment token for AI‑related services, including:
